About Me

Hi there! I'm a senior at Monta Vista High School who's interested in pursuing an education in cognitive science. Aside from my formal education, I'm an avid web developer who enjoys designing sites and games- you can see some of my projects below. I try to keep things as made-from-scratch as possible- it's more fun that way :)


I'm also frequently involved in local hackathons and coding events- I've been to 8, created award-winning projects at 4, and am currently organizing my own hackathon. In my adventures, I've also cofounded Bananium Labs, a nonprofit that provides support to help fellow aspiring student developers get started with their own projects.


Although my main focus is web development with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and TypeScript, I've worked with C# (mostly with Unity) and dabbled in some machine learning with Python (something I hope to focus on more during college). If I'm not coding, I like to read science fiction novels, rice Linux, and play golf.
