## tools
- pan
- cheese grater
- pot
- tongs
- knife
- cutting board

## ingredients
- pasta <Footnote n='1' client:idle />
- heavy whipping cream <Footnote n='2' client:idle />
- olive oil
- salt
- garlic
- black pepper
- parsley
- parmesean cheese block <Footnote n='3' client:idle />

## steps
1. bring a large pot of salty water to a boil.
2. mince garlic.
3. add pasta and cook to slightly below al dente (pasta will keep cooking in the sauce).
4. add a few tbsp's of olive oil to a pan over medium-high heat.
5. simmer garlic until slightly browned.
6. add heavy cream to the pan.
7. add pasta and a few spoons of pasta water to the pan.
8. toss pasta and sauce. add parsley, salt, and black pepper to taste.
9. turn off heat, and grate a generous amount of parmesean cheese over the pasta. mix well and serve immediately.

## troubleshooting
1. <FootnoteRef n='1' client:idle>Any wide noodle (linguine, pappardelle, tagliatelle, fettuccine) works well.</FootnoteRef>
2. <FootnoteRef n='2' client:idle>Substitute for half and half to reduce fat (but also tastiness).</FootnoteRef>
2. <FootnoteRef n='3' client:idle>Please do not use pre-grated cheese ! or i will cry !</FootnoteRef>
