Banana Cake

the best solution for overripe bananas

// vegetarian // 60min

tools

  • Large baking pan (9x13in)
    • Can also make muffins, multiple batches, or a fractional recipe if you don’t have this exact size. I do not recommend using a loaf pan, it’s too deep and might come out dry.
  • Oven
  • Small and large mixing bowls
  • Spatula
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Medium mixing bowl
  • Blender (optional)

steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mash bananas and pineapples together in a small bowl.
  3. In a large bowl, mix all dry ingredients together.`
  4. In a medium bowl, mix all wet ingredients together.
  5. Add all wet ingredients into the big bowl, and mix.
  6. Add optional ingredients if desired.
  7. Bake 40 minutes.1

ingredients

dry

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar 2
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda 3
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

wet

  • about half a can of 20oz crushed pineapple (with juice)
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2-3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 1/3 cup canola oil
  • 3 eggs

optional addons

  • chocolate chips
  • chopped pecans or other nuts
    • Don’t want to chop nuts? pulse in a blender for 0.5s

troubleshooting

  1. you will know it’s done if you stick a knife/fork/toothpick in the middle, and it comes out clean.
  2. my sweetness preference is on the low end. if you like sweet things, add an extra 1/3 to 1/2 cup sugar.
  3. didn’t rise? your baking soda might be dead, buy a new box.

pairings

  • banana cake + vanilla ice cream
  • honestly great just by itself
